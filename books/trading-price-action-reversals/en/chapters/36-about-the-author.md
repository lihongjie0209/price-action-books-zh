About the Author
Al Brooks is a technical analysis contributor to Futures magazine and an
independent day trader. Called the trader’s trader, he has a devoted following,
and provides live market commentary and daily chart analysis updates on his
website
at
www.brookspriceaction.com.
After
changing
careers
from
ophthalmology to trading 25 years ago, he discovered consistent trading success
once he developed his unique approach to reading price charts bar by bar. He
graduated from the University of Chicago Pritzker School of Medicine and
received his BS in mathematics from Trinity College.
